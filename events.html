Monthly Drop-ins

Enact hosts free, open drop-in sessions every month — online, accessible to anyone with an interest in practice research infrastructure. Sessions are informal and welcoming to all career stages and disciplines.

Each drop-in features a short project update, a community conversation topic, and plenty of time for open questions. Drop in for the whole hour or just part of it.

2026 Drop-in Programme

All sessions run 12:00–13:00 UK time via online video call. Registration links open 2 weeks before each session.

Enact 'On the Road'

Enact travels to practice research communities across the UK — bringing the project to you, hearing about your work, and building the networks and knowledge that will shape the platform. Events are free and open to all.

Glasgow 22 April 2026

An afternoon of conversation about practice research infrastructure in Scotland. Hear from the Enact team, meet pathfinder projects, and contribute to our community needs mapping. Hosted in partnership with a Glasgow institution.
