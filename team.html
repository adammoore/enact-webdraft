Enact Technology Partner

Enact works with a specialist technology partner to develop and maintain the repository platform that underpins the project's infrastructure goals.

Technology Partner

Repository Platform Development

Our technology partner brings expertise in open research infrastructure, metadata standards, and scalable repository architecture. Working closely with the Delivery Team and community, they are building the Enact platform on open standards and interoperable protocols.

The platform integrates with existing persistent identifier systems (DOI, ORCID, RAiD), supports diverse media types central to practice research, and is designed for long-term preservation and discoverability.
