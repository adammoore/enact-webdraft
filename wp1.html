Overview

WP1 establishes and maintains the governance, administrative infrastructure, and project management systems that underpin the entire Enact initiative. It ensures effective coordination across all partner institutions and maintains accountability to AHRC throughout the project lifetime.

This work package runs continuously across all three years, providing the operational backbone that enables the substantive research and community work in WP2–WP5 to proceed effectively.

Key Activities

  • Establishing the Project Board and governance structures across partner institutions
  • Setting up financial management and reporting systems
  • Developing and maintaining the project risk register
  • Coordinating regular partner meetings and cross-institutional working groups
  • Managing AHRC reporting requirements, milestones, and financial returns
  • Overseeing the project website and communications infrastructure
  • Managing data protection, ethics compliance, and open access requirements
